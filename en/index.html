TravElly is a travel diary app for kids and families. During the trip, kids save photos, stories, and memories without social media, ads, or accounts. The ideal vacation app for kids ages 6 to 14.

About TravElly

From planning to keepsake

TravElly does two things. Before the trip, kids see where you're going and what stops are ahead. It builds anticipation and sets expectations. During the trip, they make their own travel diary: pick photos, write about what they experienced, and share their day with whoever they want.

🗺️

Before the trip: build the adventure together

Fill in the destination, see where you're staying, how you'll travel. Your kid pictures the trip and counts down to departure. Great to do together on an iPad or iPhone.

📤

Share the trip with the whole family

Trip ready? Send it in one tap to anyone with an iPhone. They open the file and have the same trip in their own TravElly app, ready to start their own diary.

✍️

During the trip: your kid's diary

Kids fill in their own diary on the road: pick photos, write about their day, and share it as a postcard with grandparents or friends.

💌

Send it as a postcard

One tap turns a diary page into a digital postcard. Send it via WhatsApp, iMessage, or email. Grandparents light up, your kid feels proud.

Who it's for

Made for kids, with input from kids

An app where parents and kids work together.

TravElly is designed for kids ages 6 to 14. Parents can set up the trip and give their kids a sense of what's coming. Kids then fill in their own diary with photos and stories.

The app can live on a parent's phone, so kids can use it together or with supervision. That fits families who prefer limited or controlled smartphone use. You can also install TravElly directly on your child's phone. You decide what works for your family.

Accessibility

Comfortable for everyone

TravElly follows your iPhone's dark mode setting automatically. Dark mode was originally developed for people with light sensitivity or acquired brain injury. The calmer, darker color scheme reduces visual strain and makes the app more comfortable for anyone who benefits from softer light.

FAQ

Good to know

What age group is TravElly for?

TravElly is made for kids ages 6 to 14. Young enough to find it fun, old enough to write and pick their own photos. Younger kids can use it together with a parent.

Does it work without internet?

Yes. Everything works offline, which is ideal on vacation. You only need a connection to send a postcard.

Does it cost anything?

No. TravElly is a free download on the App Store. No subscription, no in-app purchases.

Can I share the trip with my family?

Yes. Once the trip is set up, share it via WhatsApp, AirDrop, or iMessage. Anyone with an iPhone and TravElly receives the same trip and can keep their own diary.

Can I keep multiple trips?

Yes. You can create multiple trips. Each adventure gets its own diary.

Does my child need their own phone?

No. The app can live on a parent's phone, so kids can use it together or with supervision. Or install TravElly directly on your child's phone, whichever fits your family.

Is there an Android version?

Not right now. TravElly is only available for iPhone and iPad. An Android version is on the wishlist.
